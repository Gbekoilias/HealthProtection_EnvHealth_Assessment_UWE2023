Section A of the assessment focuses on Health Protection, specifically Measles. Students are required to discuss the burden and public health impacts of Measles for a named country, describe immediate public health actions, explain how Measles is transmitted, and discuss the outbreak management in a given scenario. Additionally, they need to write a letter to parents in the context of a Measles outbreak. Section A has a total of 50 marks.

Section B addresses Ambient Air Pollution. Students must discuss health impacts, provide interventions supported by evidence, and outline information for public communication in a scenario. Section B also carries 50 marks.
